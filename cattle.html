Fresh Fodder To Stray Cattle

What a better way to celebrate birthdays, anniversaries, even festivals, than feeding the voiceless?

We feed approx 100 stray/cows and bulls in the whole of noida. You can pick and choose the date/day and contribute for the full feeding or half feeding. You can take selfie pics with the gentle cows.

You can pose with your children, grandchildren, and earn a lot of good karmas.

We make professional reports where we will write your name/family name and sent to many whatsapp groups where media people, social workers, officials are there. Come forward, book your dates.

Feed one voiceless, you will earn back ten times more.
