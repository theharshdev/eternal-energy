Our Works

We are a group of responsible citizens who have come together to work for community animals, environment and the needy.

COVID Period Works

We distributed groceries to over 3500 families during the first lockdown period. During the second lockdown period, we helped hundreds of patients with hospital beds, Oxygen cylinders, masks etc.
